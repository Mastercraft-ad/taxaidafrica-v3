Our Story

Who We Are

TaxAid and Enlightenment Initiative (TAX-AID) is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to promoting voluntary tax compliance and supporting financially vulnerable individuals and small businesses across Nigeria.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between complex tax regulations and everyday Nigerians, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality tax assistance regardless of their financial situation.

Our team of dedicated volunteers from tax practice, financial services, and academia work tirelessly to make tax accessible and understandable for all.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To promote voluntary tax compliance by providing free tax education, assistance, and advocacy for financially vulnerable individuals and small businesses in Nigeria, ensuring that everyone can fulfill their civic duty with confidence.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where every citizen understands their tax obligations and has access to the support they need to comply. We envision a society where tax compliance is not a burden but a shared responsibility that benefits all.
