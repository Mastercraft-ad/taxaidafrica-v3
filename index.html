What We Do

Our Activities

Nine core activities driving our mission to create a tax-compliant, financially literate society.

Educate

Training & Guidance

Offer training, guidance and consultancy services on tax matters.

Conferences & Workshops

Organise conferences, workshops, seminars and webinars on taxation and related issues.

Education Materials

Develop and distribute tax information, education and communication materials.

Support

Professional Tax Advisory

Provide and finance access to professional tax advisory assistance for small businesses and vulnerable taxpayers.

Government Collaboration

Collaborate with tax authorities and relevant government agencies to promote tax compliance.

Charitable Partnerships

Partner with or support charitable causes, institutions, societies and associations working to enhance tax compliance.

Advocate

Taxpayer Rights

Promote and advocate for taxpayers' rights across Nigeria.

Tax Research

Research tax-related issues and share findings with the government and the public.

Public Awareness

Enhance public awareness and education on taxation across communities.

Corporate Governance

Led by Experts

Our leadership structure ensures professional oversight and strategic direction for all our programmes.

Advisory Board

Strategic Leadership

TAX-AID is governed by an Advisory Board comprising reputable professionals from the taxation and broader business community. The Board provides strategic guidance and approves the organisation's programmes.

Tax Experts Business Leaders Industry Professionals

Executive Council

Day-to-Day Management

The Advisory Board appoints an Executive Council responsible for day-to-day management, programme implementation, and ensuring alignment with the organisation's strategic goals.
